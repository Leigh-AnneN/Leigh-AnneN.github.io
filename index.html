About me

My journey into software engineering began in high school when I discovered my passion for programming during computer science classes. Despite having an unusual mix of interests in maths, art, and computers, I found myself drawn to the world of coding.

Over the years, my path took me through various roles, from updating and managing websites for galleries to even owning my art gallery. As I navigated the arts management domain, my fascination with software engineering persisted, leading me to solidify my skills in the field.

Currently, I'm actively building applications aimed at easing the workload for those in non-profit organizations, an area where I can merge my passion for technology and dedication to making a positive impact.

Through my experiences and continuous learning, I've cultivated strong communication, project management, and problem-solving skills. As a result, I thrive in collaborative environments and enjoy applying creative solutions to complex challenges.

I am excited to embark on this new chapter and leverage my diverse background to contribute to innovative software projects while continually seeking opportunities for growth and knowledge in the ever-evolving world of technology.
